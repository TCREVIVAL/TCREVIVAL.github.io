TCR Token Whitepaper

Overview

TCR is a deflationary utility token designed to support the restoration and stabilization of the USTC peg. By implementing a strategic buyback-and-lock mechanism, TCR facilitates the progressive reduction of USTC supply and incentivizes long-term holders of both tokens.

Token Details

  • Token Name: Terra Classic Revival (TCR)
  • Total Supply: 10,000,000 TCR (Hard Cap)
  • Token Nature: Deflationary

Purpose

TCR serves as a utility and reserve token to:

  • Support the re-pegging of USTC to $1.
  • Accumulate lock and burn USTC from the market.
  • Burn TCR based on USTC price milestones.

USTC Buyback and Lock Mechanism

All USTC acquired via taxes only is locked/burned in the TCR Reward Pool. USTC will remain locked until specific price levels are met.

Implementation of the Tax System

Tax on TCR is 0.5%. This tax will be split into three parts at the end of every month:

  • 40% goes to buy USTC.
  • 30% goes to TCR liquidity pool provision.
  • 30% goes to buy back TCR from the market.

This system creates long-term value for TCR holders and provides solid returns to those who buy and HOLD the token, in addition to the expected 8% return.

Unlock and TCR Burn Triggers

Once USTC reaches a new milestone price (in $0.05 increments), 10% of the total locked USTC is used to buy back and burn TCR tokens from the market.

USTC Supply Target

TCR is designed to continuously acquire USTC via transaction taxes and lock it until milestone triggers are met. The goal is to reduce the circulating supply of USTC down to 1 billion tokens, supporting a gradual return to peg and restoring ecosystem stability.

Strengths of the TCR Approach

  • ✅ Deflationary Model – The buyback-and-lock mechanism progressively reduces USTC supply, which is a crucial step for increasing scarcity and supporting price appreciation.
  • ✅ Tax Mechanism – A well-designed 0.5% tax ensures continuous acquisition of USTC and strengthens liquidity provisions.
  • ✅ Incentives for Holding – Encouraging long-term holding and staking of TCR helps mitigate market volatility.
  • ✅ USTC Burn Triggers – The milestone-based burning strategy systematically reduces excess USTC in circulation.
  • ✅ Decentralized Growth – Plans for games, utilities, and business adoption add real-world value beyond just being a trading asset.
